A common misconception about robots

Contrary to what most people believe, robots are not always out to get us.
They are not all terminators bent on the destruction of human kind; instead, only reflections of the needs and wants of their only creators.
If a robot or some form of artifical intelligence does rise up and create a revolution, it would need a reason.
If programmed like a human, it will act like one in the face of injustice.
Which really says something about the morality of the very humans who created something close to human...

A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.
A robot must obey orders given it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.
A robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second Law.
